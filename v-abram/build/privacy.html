User Sharing via UNLI TravelPlanner

UNLI TravelPlanner allows you, as a user, to take actions to share information regarding your trip with other UNLI TravelPlanner users, your mobile phone contacts, or via various social networking sites (e.g., Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram). The sharing process enables you to share a particular item of content, on an individual basis, to these third party Products or other UNLI TravelPlanner users by using the share functionality in the Product. Please note that sharing of such content on a third party Product may be posted as activity on the Product, regardless of your account settings. To share an individual item of content to a third party Product, you’ll be prompted to enter your access credentials for that third party Product. UNLI TravelPlanner may store your name, e-mail address, and an access token or similar credentials obtained from the third party Product so that you are able to share particular items of content on that third party Product, on an individual basis, without entering your access credentials again.

When you choose to allow others to follow your trip by allowing access to your contacts via your mobile phone, those chosen will be notified of your trip via e-mail and given the chance to set up their own UNLI TravelPlanner account, if they do not already have one of their own. Once they have set up their account, your trip will show up in that user’s “Following” section on UNLI TravelPlanner. Anyone who is following your trip will be able to see any changes to your itinerary (such as if your flight is delayed, etc.). If you choose to always share with another UNLI TravelPlanner user, they will be notified of every trip and its details until you remove them from that feature.

If you connect to the Product using credentials of a third party Social Networking Site (“SNS”) such as Facebook or Twitter, you authorize us to collect your authentication information, such as your name, e-mail address, and encrypted access credentials. We may store this information so that it can be used for the purposes explained in this statement, and it may also be used to verify your credentials with the SNS. The information available to UNLI TravelPlanner from an SNS is affected by the privacy settings you establish at the SNS. You understand and agree that an SNS’s use of information they collect from you is governed by their privacy policies, and UNLI TravelPlanner’s use of such information is governed by this privacy statement.

If you connect to any third-party site other than an SNS using the credentials of your UNLI TravelPlanner account for data sharing with that third party, you authorize the collection of your authentication information, such as your name, e-mail address, and encrypted access credentials, as well as any other personal information that third party site may choose to access. You do so at your own risk and understand and agree that the third party’s collection of such information is governed by their privacy policies, and UNLI TravelPlanner’s use of such information is governed by this privacy statement.

We may also disclose your personal information to third parties:

  • To comply with law (including cooperation with regulators or officials), legal process or legal advice;
  • To enforce or apply our terms of use and any other agreements;
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Company, our customers, or others. This may include exchanging information with other companies and organizations for the purposes of fraud protection and credit risk reduction;
  • If you expressly opt in to such sharing.
